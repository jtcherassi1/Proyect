Developed by Soundlux Audio, Miami’s premier hi-fi studio, Ura’s system will feature high-end modern and vintage audio equipment. It will be designed expressly to Ura’s restaurant space in order to amplify guests’ emotional connection to the music and harmonize the sound, sushi, and space into one enduring and extraordinary sensory experience. The music genres and individual song selections will vary throughout the dining experience, shifting with the vibe and mood of the evening, while remaining both relaxed and atmospheric.
