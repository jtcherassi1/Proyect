Our Team
Samuel Tcherassi Partner For the past 10 years, Samuel has been working for his family’s business, EPK, the second largest children’s clothing brand in Colombia which boasts over 75 store locations. As Executive Vice President of EPK, Samuel has been instrumental in the brand’s persistent growth and outstanding success, providing consistent and effective leadership, astute decision making and management, and innovative and strategic ideas for growth.
Jose Tcherassi Partner Though currently finishing his studies at Babson College, José Tcherassi formally joined the family business in 2020. Working under the design and sales divisions of the company, Jose has specialized on the relationship between intelligent design and customer satisfaction. A lifelong passion for the arts allowed José to ideate Ura’s brand concept, and his formation at the number-one school for entrepreneurship will assist the venture’s commercial success.
